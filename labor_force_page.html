Forecasting the U.S. Male Labor Force

As part of my Macro Forecasting course, I developed a seasonal ARIMA (SARIMA) model to analyze and forecast the U.S. male labor force using data from 1948 to 2025. This extensive time series dataset, pulled from the Bureau of Labor Statistics, served as the foundation for training and testing predictive accuracy.

I tuned the SARIMA model to capture both trend and seasonality in the labor force, achieving a backtesting error of less than 1% when applied to prior months.
